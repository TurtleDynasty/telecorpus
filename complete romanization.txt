Capital letter tee (T) represents the shape of the agent entity in a message sequence diagram. 
eg "Tkorp" means body or person. The T is not being pronounced. It is a markup symbol. Like punctuation. 
Dash (-) represents the tail end of the arrow attached to message sender agent. 
Dash and right-pointing angle bracket (-> represents the tip of the arrow attached to message reciever agent. 
alphabetic notation representing a message goes in between these markup symbols. 
eg "-mone->" means giving money.

Phrase example
"Tkorp-mone->Tkorp." means A person gives money to another person.

Full sentence example
"Tkorp-mone->Tkorp-mant->Tkorp." means "I buy food."
